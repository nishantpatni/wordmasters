────────────────────────────────────────
COLLECTIVE NOUNS — Extra Practice added
────────────────────────────────────────
35 hand-picked tricky questions are now available as extra practice
for Collective Nouns. These cover commonly confused and lesser-known
collective nouns (e.g. a troop of lions/monkeys, a shoal of fish,
a flight of stairs, a string of camels/pearls/beads).

Extra practice questions always appear first in your session, so
you'll tackle the trickiest ones before moving to the full set.

────────────────────────────────────────
SYNONYMS — Smarter, harder distractors
────────────────────────────────────────
Synonym questions now use words from the same meaning family as the
wrong choices. For example, if the answer is "Serene", the options
might be Placid, Tranquil, and Composed — not random unrelated words.

This makes the questions genuinely challenging and helps you learn
the fine differences between similar words.
