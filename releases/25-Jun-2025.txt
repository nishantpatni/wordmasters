Word Masters — Release Notes
25 June 2025
============================================================

WHAT'S NEW
----------

🎤  Voice Quiz (5 topics)
    A brand-new quiz mode where you speak your answers instead of tapping.
    Available for: Idioms, One Word Substitutions, Proverbs, Oxymorons,
    and Similes.

    How it works:
    • The app reads the clue aloud (e.g. the meaning of an idiom).
    • You speak the answer — the mic picks it up and checks it.
    • Auto-submit is on by default: as soon as you stop talking, your
      answer is evaluated. You can turn this off to review/edit the
      transcript first, or re-record.
    • Correct answers need to match ~90% of the words (small typos /
      speech-to-text errors are forgiven).
    • After each answer you'll hear the correct phrase spoken aloud,
      and see each word coloured green (matched) or red (missed).
    • Press Enter to submit in manual mode.
    • Works in Chrome on desktop only (uses the Web Speech API).

    To start: pick any of the 5 supported topics → tap the 🎤 50 or
    🎤 100 button.


🗣️  MCQ Voice Input (hands-free mode)
    You can now answer the regular multiple-choice quiz by voice too.
    Enable it in the quiz settings panel (⚙️ button, top-left).

    How it works:
    • After the question term is read aloud, the mic activates.
    • Say a number — "one", "two", "three", or "four" — to pick that
      option.
    • Or just say the answer text; the app matches your words to the
      closest option.
    • If nothing matches, you'll hear a double-beep and a "try again"
      prompt — then the mic restarts automatically.
    • Disabled automatically on multi-select questions.
    • Works in Chrome on desktop only.


⚙️  Quiz Settings Panel
    A new ⚙️ button appears in the top-left of every quiz. Tap it to
    access:
    • Speak key term — read the question word/phrase aloud at the
      start of each question (was the 🔊 button; now also here).
    • Speak correct answer — after each correct pick, hear the answer
      spoken aloud. On by default.
    • 🎤 Voice input — hands-free MCQ answering (see above).
    All settings are remembered between sessions.


🎉  Affirmative Animations
    Correct answers now celebrate with a random encouraging message
    that pops up and floats away:
    "Nice one!", "You nailed it!", "Spot on!", "Boom, correct!",
    "Well played!", "That's a win!" — 20 phrases in rotation.


📝  Quiz Button Icons
    The 50 and 100 question buttons now show 📝 (MCQ) or 🎤 (Voice)
    icons so it's clear at a glance which mode you're launching.


BUG FIX
-------
    Underscores in question prompts (e.g. "Complete: Act ___") are
    now correctly pronounced as "blank" by the text-to-speech engine
    instead of being read as "underscore underscore underscore".
